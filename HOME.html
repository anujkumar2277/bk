Founded in 2009, B.K. is a rapidly growing Sr. Sec. School in Jahangirabad run by Kusum Devi Educational and Social Welfare Society Jahangirabad. (Distt. Bulandshahr) Our History B.K. Senior Secondary School was founded in Jahangirabad for the need of the quality based education and upliftment of the children for their all round growth and development and was upgraded in 2012 up to +2 level in Science and Commerce streams.B.K. Public Senior Secondary School, a unique name in education and discipline is affiliated to CBSE and is run by Kusum Devi Educational and Social Welfare Society Jahangirabad. Our Location The school is located at Binner Devi road, Jahangirabad in lush green surrounding with state of the art infrastructure and away from the hustle-bustle of the cities. Its calm and peaceful environment presents a very pious and soothing sight to the onlookers.We at B.K. are providing co-education to the learners of Jahangirabad & adjoining areas.The institution emphasizes maximally the physical, mental, moral, intellectual, sociological,and spiritual development of a child. The chastity of character is the gems which we are sowing & nurturing among the students.
